About PaintingNow Group

Connecting Australians with trusted painters for over 25 years

Our Story

Since 1999, PaintingNow Group has been Australia's most trusted painting network, connecting homeowners and businesses with qualified, vetted painting professionals across Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, and the Gold Coast.

We started with a simple mission: to eliminate the stress and uncertainty of finding a reliable painter. Too many Australians were struggling with unreliable tradespeople, unclear quotes, and disappointing results. We knew there had to be a better way.

Over the past 25+ years, we've built relationships with hundreds of professional painters, carefully vetting each one to ensure they meet our high standards of quality, reliability, and professionalism. We've facilitated thousands of successful painting projects, from small residential touch-ups to large commercial renovations.

What Sets Us Apart

Unlike other painting services, we don't employ painters ourselves. Instead, we've created a curated network of independent painting professionals who have proven their expertise through years of exceptional work. This means you get the personalized service of working with an independent tradesperson, backed by the quality assurance of our rigorous vetting process.

Every painter in our network is fully licensed, insured, and has been independently verified. We conduct thorough background checks, review past work, and regularly collect feedback from customers to ensure our standards remain consistently high.

When you request a quote through PaintingNow Group, we match you with painters who specialize in your specific type of project and location. Whether you need interior painting in a Sydney terrace, exterior work on a Melbourne warehouse, or a complete commercial renovation in Brisbane, we connect you with the right specialist for the job.
